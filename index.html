About me

I am a second year PhD student in Economics at the HEC Lausanne, University of Lausanne. I am co-supervised by Dominic Rohner and Ekaterina Zhuravskaya.

My research interests are Applied Microeconomics, Development Economics, Political Economics and Economics of Religion.

I graduated from Paris School of Economics where I wrote my thesis on impact of political quotas on the socio-economics status of minorities under the supervision of Ekaterina Zhuravskaya.
