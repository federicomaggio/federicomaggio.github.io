About me

I am a first year PhD student in Economics at the London School of Economics, and an affiliate researcher at the Collège de France.

My research interests are technological change, human capital, inequality and entrepreneurship. As a hobby, I model human behavior and bounded rationality.

I graduated from Paris School of Economics where I wrote my thesis on long run inequality of opportunity under the supervision of Thomas Piketty.
